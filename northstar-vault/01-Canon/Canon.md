# Canon

> [!info] Why this page exists
> Every company accumulates identity — mission, values, how it talks about itself, who's on it, what's happened. Left undocumented, that identity lives only in the founder's head and gets rewritten from memory every time someone asks "wait, why do we do it that way?" The Canon is Northstar's answer to that question, written down once and kept authoritative.

> [!tip] When to use this page
> Read the Canon during onboarding, before writing anything public-facing (marketing, job posts, investor updates), or whenever a decision seems to hinge on "who we are" rather than "what's technically correct." If a [[ADR Index|decision]] conflicts with something here, that's worth surfacing explicitly, not quietly overriding.

> [!note] How it connects to the rest of the vault
> The Canon is the top of the vault's story (see [[Vault Guide]]) — it's the "who/why" that every decision in [[ADR Index]], and every future section added per [[Vault Guide#How the vault grows]], should be consistent with. It's named `Canon.md` rather than `Canon Index.md` deliberately: this folder is less a list of interchangeable notes and more a small, fixed set of foundational documents, so it reads as a title, not a query result.
