# People & Org

> [!info] Why this page exists
> "Who owns this?" is one of the most common questions in any growing company. This page is the canonical answer — a lightweight org reference that scales from "it's just me" to a multi-team structure without needing to be rebuilt.

> [!tip] When to use this page
> When assigning an `owner` field in frontmatter (see [[Conventions]]) and you're unsure who that should resolve to. When onboarding someone new, to understand reporting lines and team boundaries.

> [!note] How it connects to the rest of the vault
> Every `owner` field across the vault (ADRs, specs, systems, runbooks) should resolve to a person or role listed here. [[06-Meetings/1-1s/1-1s Index|1:1 hub pages]] link back to the person's entry here.
