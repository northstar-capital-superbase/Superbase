# Competitive Research

> [!info] Why this page exists
> Knowing what alternatives exist — and what they get right or wrong — sharpens product decisions. This folder is a durable, structured place for that, rather than scattered browser bookmarks and half-remembered demos.

> [!tip] When to use this page
> Log a note (from [[Research Note Template]]) per competitor or per significant competitive event (a launch, a pricing change). Revisit before writing a [[Specs Index|spec]] in a space where competitors already operate.

> [!note] How it connects to the rest of the vault
> Should inform [[Specs Index|specs]] and strategic conversations reflected in [[Roadmap]]. Keep it factual and current — stale competitive notes are actively misleading.
