# User Research

> [!info] Why this page exists
> Direct evidence of what users actually need, say, and do is the highest-signal input to product decisions — and the easiest to lose if it's not captured right after an interview or usability session.

> [!tip] When to use this page
> Log a note (from [[Research Note Template]]) right after any user interview, usability test, or survey analysis — while it's fresh. Reference findings when creating or revising a [[Personas Index|persona]].

> [!note] How it connects to the rest of the vault
> This is the evidentiary base for [[Personas Index]] — a persona should be traceable to research here, not invented. Also feeds [[Feedback Index]] (though feedback is unsolicited/continuous, this is structured/deliberate research).
