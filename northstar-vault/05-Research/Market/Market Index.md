# Market Research

> [!info] Why this page exists
> Understanding of the broader market — trends, regulation, size, timing — shapes strategy but is easy to lose track of once it's read once and never written down. This folder is where that context gets recorded so it's revisitable.

> [!tip] When to use this page
> Log a note (from [[Research Note Template]]) after any significant market research effort. Reference it when writing strategic sections of [[Roadmap]] or [[Mission & Vision]] discussions.

> [!note] How it connects to the rest of the vault
> Distinct from [[Competitive Index]] (specific competitors) — this is the broader industry/market context those competitors operate within.
